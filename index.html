The Band

Shouts, screams, growls, and cleans... and all from two voices that couldn't be more different. ASURA FALLS consists of 6 passionate musicians from the heart of Europe, delivering melodic riffs, deep basslines, drums that make any stage shake, and a diverse front line that turns the world of metalcore upside down. Inspired by genre giants like Bullet For My Valentine and Killswitch Engage, a killer evening is guaranteed for every fan of the heavier side!

Jenny - Vocals

Even as a young child, Jenny liked to be on stage, no matter where she was, if she liked a song, she sang along and danced to it. In her youth, she was inspired by Linkin Park, Green Day, Bullet for my Valentine. Her current role models include "In This Moment" singer Maria Brink and "Beyond the Black" singer Jennifer Haben. With her engaging stage presence and convincing voice ranging from gentle and empathetic to heavy and aggressive. She joined this year in February as the last member and made us complete with our sound.

Nemo - Vocals

Nemo discovered his love for music at an early age and now, many years later, he has been the frontman of Asura Falls. With many years of experience as a shouter, rapper and lyricist, he provides the necessary hardness with his unmistakable style and voice and moves forward without compromise.

Jonas - 8-String

Inspired by his music teacher, Jonas started his musical career relatively late, but all the more determined. His journey and with it the search for his own sound began with influences such as In Flames, Caliban, and Insomnium. Armed with 8 strings and his songwriting, Jonas lays a foundation of heaviness and melancholy on which the band builds its songs. Even if he is rarely in the foreground, he is irreplaceable as a rhythm guitarist and “the band's own tech guy” and contributes significantly to the band's sound.

Cons - 7-String

Since the very beginning, Cons has been a founding member, lead guitarist and therefore the “veteran” of our band.He has been playing guitar since he was 8 years old and his influences from classic blues and metal make him a significant contributor to the typical “Asura sound”. Inspired by modern core bands, he transforms ideas into melodic riffs and accompanies them with synth sounds.

Sueri - Bass

Sueri discovered his passion for deep tones at the age of 14 and his roots in the early 2000s skatepunk. After a long break, he has been an integral member of Asura Falls since 2016 and, as the esteemed bassist of our band, provides the essential foundation that allows our music to be felt.

Erausmus - Drums

While playing multiple instruments, his true passion lies in the drums. Drawing inspiration from a wide range of styles—from game soundtracks to progressive metal—his playing blends heavy beats with playful fills, creating a distinctive energy and drive.
