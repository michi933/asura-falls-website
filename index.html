The Band

Asura Falls stand for high-energy modern metalcore with a clear message, massive riffs, and two unmistakable voices. The six-piece band combines melodic depth, emotional intensity, and uncompromising heaviness into a unique sound that truly gets under your skin. With influences from Bullet For My Valentine, Killswitch Engage, and In Flames, Asura Falls create a powerful blend of catchy melodies, aggressive breakdowns, and atmospheric moments. Clean vocals and distinctive shouts/growls meet modern walls of guitars and driving drums — a sound that convinces just as much live on stage as it does in the studio. Since the relaunch with Jenny and Nemo on vocals, the band has gained a fresh dynamic. Together with Jonas (8-string guitar), Cons (7-string lead guitar), Sueri (bass), and Erausmus (drums), they form a powerful collective that unites heart, heaviness, and attitude. Whether on big stages or in intimate club shows, Asura Falls stand for authenticity, passion, and electrifying live energy. The band transforms small clubs and large stages alike into a sea of movement, emotion, and pure energy — proving that metalcore can be far more than just volume. With new songs and upcoming shows, Asura Falls are ready for the next step — uncompromising, honest, and driven by a clear goal: to bring their music to the world.

Jenny - Vocals

Even as a small child, Jenny loved being on stage — no matter where she was. If she liked a song, she would sing along and dance to it. During her teenage years, her musical influences included bands such as Linkin Park, Green Day, and Bullet For My Valentine. Today, artists like Maria Brink of In This Moment and Jennifer Haben of Beyond the Black are among her inspirations. With her captivating stage presence and a compelling voice that ranges from soft and sensitive to powerful and aggressive, she brings tremendous dynamic energy to the band.

Nemo - Vocals

Nemo discovered his passion for music at an early age and, many years later, is now the frontman of Asura Falls. With his long-standing experience as a shouter, rapper, and lyricist, he gives the band the heaviness it needs. Through his unmistakable style and distinctive voice, he drives the sound forward without compromise.

Jonas - 8-String

Inspired by his music teacher, Jonas began his musical journey relatively late — but all the more determined. His path — and with it the search for his own sound — was shaped by influences from bands such as In Flames, Caliban, and Insomnium. Armed with eight strings and his songwriting skills, Jonas lays a foundation of heaviness and melancholy on which the band builds its songs. Even though he rarely stands in the spotlight, he is indispensable as the rhythm guitarist and the band’s in-house “tech guy,” making a crucial contribution to their overall sound.

Cons - 7-String

Since day one, Cons has been a founding member, lead guitarist, and therefore the “veteran” of our band. He has been playing guitar since the age of eight, and his influences from classic blues and metal have played a major role in shaping the distinctive Asura sound. Inspired by modern core bands, he transforms ideas into melodic riffs and enhances them with atmospheric synth elements.

Sueri - Bass

Sueri discovered his passion for low tones at the age of 14 — his roots lie in the skate punk of the early 2000s. After a longer break, he has been a permanent member of Asura Falls since 2016 and, as the band’s valued bassist, provides the essential foundation that makes our music truly felt.

Erasmus - Drums

Although he masters several instruments, his true passion lies behind the drums. Influenced by a wide range of styles — from video game soundtracks to progressive metal — his playing combines powerful beats with playful fills, creating a distinctive energy and dynamic feel.
